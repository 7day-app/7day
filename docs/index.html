7day

the weekly average is your true weight

What it does

  • Daily weigh-ins, weekly averages. Your weight fluctuates day to day. 7day smooths the noise by calculating weekly averages so you can see what's actually happening.
  • Plan your blocks. Set up cutting, bulking, or maintenance cycles with goals. See at a glance whether you're on track each week.
  • Track real trends. Charts and weekly breakdowns show your progress over weeks and months, not daily noise.

How it works

1
Step on the scale Weigh yourself most mornings. Open 7day and log it in under 3 seconds.
2
See your weekly average The app calculates your Monday-to-Sunday average automatically. That number is your real weight.
3
Watch the trend Weekly averages plotted over time show the true direction. No more stressing over daily swings.
